About this example

This is a more advanced example to show the multiple usage of .page_margins and .page to create flexible full-page layouts in combination with YAML's powerful subtemplates.

The upper #main content area (white background) is subdivided into three sections of 50% | 25% | 25% width.

The lower #extended content area (gray background) is subdivided in the same way, but in addition uses YAML's equal heights solution to get these full height vertical column dividers.
